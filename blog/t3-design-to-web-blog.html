Responsive web design and its importance

Responsive design caters to organising development of websites keeping various interfaces in mind. With development of several devices ranging from mobile phones to large desktops, users are interacting with websites with different screen resolutions. This enables a better user experience thereby ensuring access to your websites by maximum number of people.

Importance of mobile first design

A Mobile first approach ensures your designs caters to the lowest screen resolution first and then experience can be enhanced with increasing screen size. This approach eases designing and eliminates possible problems that may arise by reversing the process of designing for a bigger screen resolution to a smaller one. This ensure better User Interface and User Experience.

What frameworks are, and their pros and cons. What a wireframe is and why we use it.

Frameworks are bridges that help code faster and easier. However not all approaches to framework could mean a better way to code and build web sites. However one cannot reduce the importance of framework as it acts a great planning tool and gives a headstart to designing websites.

Frameworks increases your efficiency of coding, are cost effective as most frameworks are free and come with excellent documentation ensuring secure way of developing websites.

Though frameworks are very efficient, all frameworks have some or the other limitation. The core of a framework gets hard to modify and one may be forced to accept it as is. Also developers may develop the tendency of depending on frameworks may master it, but may not know the language behind the framework.

Wireframe is a grid based framework used to place various HTML elements on a page. It helps determine the exact placement of elements and acts as planning tool ensuring percentage based division of the total available space. Wireframes could be simply understood as your vision of the web elements on a grid paper.
