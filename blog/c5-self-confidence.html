Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness and self awareness mean the same. In a mindful state a person can be aware of his emotions, look at himself and do an accurate assessment and get a sense of his self worth and capabilities. Both necessarily require a person to look at himself as a third person looking at his mind.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

Practicing Journaling was an amazing experience. I used one of the prompts to write whatever I felt. It was for the first time I was just typing whatever came to my mind. This exercise helped me be completely aware of what I was feeling at that particular moment. A regular practice would open up insights into my strengths and weaknesses and help me correct myself towards being better person.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I am not my mind. Simply put, I am an entity independent of the mind who can watch the mind. This watcher awakens a reality that mind and body both are separate from the 'I' or 'Me'. All emotions which are a part of mind and body phenomena are not who we really are. This awareness shifts consciousness from "I am angry" to "I feel anger in my body". The I attached to the emotion disappears.
