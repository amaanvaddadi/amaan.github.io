Self Control

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

This chapter explains the various methods to gain self control, which in turn would increase self confidence. Self regulation with the understanding that we have a choice on how to respond to a particular stimulus helps take control of the stimulus in a better way. This chapter explains the profound wisdom of letting go and explains that excellently by the "Siberian North Rail Road" technique where humans can control distress by looking at it deeply, pausing after the trigger and reflect on how to respond. It also explains other ways to respond like taking deep creaths, counting to ten and excusing oneself from such situations.

The techniques so mentioned if adapted rightfully would enhance peace,happiness and self confidence.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

Mindfullness exercise is an enriching one. I have been practicing mindfullness and have realised several aspects of the deep consciousness that pervades all living beings. I have achieved a great deal of calm, better understanding of the situations and people I deal with. This clarity has helped me become a more compassionate human, happier individual and free of worries. It has slowly started liberating me.
